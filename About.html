Labex Databank

Our Mission

Labex is a non-profit initiative dedicated to providing the Iranian laboratory community with free and open access to comprehensive product information, facilitating informed decision-making and fostering transparency in the industry.

Our Vision

Labex envisions a future where every lab professional in Iran has free, direct access to the knowledge and tools they need — no walls, no middlemen, just progress.
